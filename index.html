How It Works

Four moves. One crown.

1

Register at Cookies DC

Sign up with your phone — 30 seconds. Pick your team name. Your passport goes live immediately.

2

Walk the Corridor

Hit stops across U Street — restaurants, bars, and cultural landmarks. No car needed. The whole route is walkable.

3

Collect Stamps

Each stop has a secret word. Get it from staff (or find it at cultural landmarks). Enter it in the app. Earn points. Learn the history.

4

Compete & Win

Live leaderboard. 3 prize rounds (4–6pm, 6–8pm, 8–10pm). Team competition. After party at Chi-Cha for passport holders.
