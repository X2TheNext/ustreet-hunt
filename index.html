How It Works

Four moves. One crown.

1

Register

Sign up with your phone number. Get your digital passport. It takes 30 seconds.

2

Explore

Hit any of the 11 checkpoints on U Street. Spend money, support the block, scan the QR code at the counter.

3

Collect

Every scan earns points + a digital stamp. Every 3 stamps = 1 free spin on the prize wheel.

4

Claim

Hit point thresholds for prizes. Spin the wheel for instant rewards. Finish all 11 stops → finale raffle at Chi-Cha.
