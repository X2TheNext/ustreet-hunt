The Concept

What is the U Street Passport?

A one-day cultural corridor experience anchored at Cookies DC. Participants register on their phone, walk U Street, collect stamps at each stop by entering a secret word from your staff, and compete on a live leaderboard for prizes. Every stop = foot traffic with purchase intent.

📲

Players Register

Sign up with a phone number. No app download. Passport lives in the browser. They see your stop on the map before they even leave home.

📍

They Walk to You

Your business is listed as a checkpoint stop. The hunt sends players to your specific address with a reason to walk in the door.

🗝️

Staff Give the Word

Your staff share one secret word with players who come in. Player enters it in the app, earns their stamp. Zero setup, zero friction.

🎁

They Redeem Your Perk

You set the offer. A free item, a discount, priority seating — whatever makes sense for your business. Real conversions, not just impressions.

🍪

Common Questions

Straight answers.

Do I need to change anything about how I operate?

No. All you need is for one staff member to know the secret word. When a hunter walks in and asks for it, they give it. That's the entire staff requirement.

What perk should I offer?

Whatever works for your margins. The most popular: free side with any purchase (restaurants), 10% off (retail/cannabis), free entry before 10pm (bars/lounges), free tea/small upgrade (hookah). We'll suggest one if you're unsure — just reach out.

What does "donate a prize to the spin wheel" mean?

Every 3 stamps a player earns = 1 free spin on the prize wheel. If you donate a prize (a free item, gift card, discount) we add a slot on the wheel with your business name. Every spin, players see your brand. It's a free slot — we just need the donated item confirmed before July 4.

Is the event really just one day?

Yes. Saturday, July 11, 4pm–10pm. We concentrated it intentionally — one big activation creates energy, competition, and FOMO. More players move faster when there's a real deadline. The corridor will feel it.

Is this real?

The passport app is live and built. Supabase backend is running. Cookies DC is the anchor partner. You can see the live app at ustreet-hunt.atcheofficial.com. The event happens regardless — the question is whether your door is on the map.
