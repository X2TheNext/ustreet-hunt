The Concept

A scavenger hunt that
pays your rent.

Participants download a digital passport, register with their phone, and spend the week of July 11–18 hitting stops along U Street. Every sponsor location is a checkpoint. Hunters scan a QR code at your counter, earn stamps and points, and unlock a perk you set — a discount, a free item, early access, whatever works for your business. The more stops they hit, the better their prizes. The more they hit yours, the more they spend with you.

The Bigger Picture

U Street wins together.

This isn't just a marketing event. The U Street Hunt exists because the corridor's businesses don't benefit from each other the way they should. You're all within walking distance, serving overlapping customers, but most nights someone picks one spot and stays there. The Hunt changes that — it creates a reason to move, to explore, to spend across multiple doors in a single night.

When a hunter gets stamped at your location and earns enough points to unlock a prize at the next stop, they don't go home. They keep walking the block. Every sponsor benefits from every other sponsor's foot traffic. The corridor rising together is the whole point.

This hunt is the first public demonstration that we can organize the corridor, move real people through it, and make the whole thing measurable. Your sponsorship is proof that U Street businesses invest in their own block.

Common Questions

Straight answers.

Do I need to change anything about how I operate?

No. We deliver a printed QR card before July 11. You display it at your counter. When a hunter scans it, they see your perk and your checkpoint is stamped. That's it — no software, no training, no friction for your staff.

What if I don't know what perk to offer?

We'll suggest one based on your business type. The goal is something with low cost to you and high perceived value to a customer. A free item on a purchase, a percentage off, or early entry tend to work well. We figure it out together before the hunt launches.

What does ATCHÉ actually do?

ATCHÉ is a community platform for creative women in DC nightlife — photographers, performers, hosts, DJs, stylists. We run activations, produce content, and build the infrastructure that connects U Street's creative and business scenes. This hunt is our first public corridor activation.

I've never heard of this. Is it real?

Yes. The passport app is live, the Supabase backend is built, Cookies DC is the anchor partner, and the launch date is July 11. You can view the live app at ustreet-hunt.atcheofficial.com. The Hunt happens regardless — the only question is whether your door is on the map.

What happens after July 18?

You get a full scan report — total hunters who visited, peak days and times, repeat scans. Partner and Presenting sponsors get photo content from their location. And if it works, there's a second hunt in Q4 — sponsors from this one get first right of refusal.
