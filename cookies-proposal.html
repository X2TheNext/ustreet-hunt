The concept

Pokémon GO for U Street.

Players register on their phone, get a digital passport, and spend July 11 hitting stops across the U Street corridor. Each business gives them a secret word — they enter it in the app, earn stamps and points, and compete on a live leaderboard. Cookies DC is home base: where you register, where you finish, where you redeem prizes.

No permits. No infrastructure. No risk. Just a community moving through the corridor for one night — and everyone benefits.
